Dumbell Curls

  1. Hold a dumbbell in both hands, and stand with your feet shoulder width apart.
  2. Face your palms forward and stand tall, slightly bending knees.
  3. Curl arms upwards until in front of shoulders
Remember not to move your elbows!

Hammer Curls

  1. Stand with your torso upright and elbows close to the torso, with a dumbell in both hands.
  2. Hold your upper arm stationary, and curl the lower arm until dumbbells are at shoulder level.
  3. Hold the contraction, and slowly lower to rest.
Remember not to move your elbows!

Bench Press

  1. Lie on the bench with eyes at bar, grab the bar with thumbs arounds it.
  2. Straighten arms to unrack weight.
  3. Lower bar to mid-chest,
  4. Press the bar back up.
Keep your arms shoulder width apart!
